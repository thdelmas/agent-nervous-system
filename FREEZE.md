## Re-decided 2026-07-16 — freeze ended, with cause

The freeze ends by explicit re-decision, seventeen days in, twelve days before its
expected trigger. Recording why, because a commitment device that ends early owes
its ledger an explanation:

1. **Its premises died.** The freeze was designed as experiment isolation while an
   income floor covered survival ("a bet, not survival") and discretionary hours were
   scarce — the scarcity is what made build-vs-sell an either/or. In mid-July both
   premises failed at once: the floor collapsed and the hour-scarcity dissolved with it.
   A freeze that outlives its premises is inertia, not discipline.
2. **It bundled two rules with different lifetimes.** (a) Experiment isolation for the
   external commercial test — *that rule survives, unchanged, until the test's gate
   resolves in late July.* (b) A global ban on intrinsic builds — alibi-removal, already
   exempted three times for organism-driven extensions. Rule (b) is what ends today.

What the freeze taught is kept as a standing norm: expansion here stays **demand-pulled**
(operational pain + a consuming host — the 2026-06-23 anti-proliferation gate), never
metaphor-pulled. Nearly everything that shipped "through" the freeze was pulled by a
real external need; that filter outlives the freeze that revealed it.
